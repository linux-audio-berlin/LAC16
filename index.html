Linux Audio Conference 2016

Welcome to the Linux Audio Conference (LAC) 2016 Github page. Here you'll find some information on the current planning status of the conference, which will take place in Berlin.

Head over to the issue tracker to help us solve all things concerning the LAC2016.

Or join #lac2016 on freenode or use Freenode's webchat client, to contact us directly over IRC.

Bi-weekly meetings take place at c-base, as part of the Linux Audio Users Group Berlin.

We also run a mailing list, to which you can subscribe.
